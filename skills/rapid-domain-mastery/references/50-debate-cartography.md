# Debate Cartography

For each major disagreement include:

- exact question under dispute
- main positions
- strongest argument for each side
- evidence or logic each side relies on
- hidden assumptions
- dispute type:
  - empirical
  - conceptual
  - methodological
  - normative
- what evidence would weaken each side

## Important
Do not caricature one side to make the other look clever.
Represent steel-man versions.
