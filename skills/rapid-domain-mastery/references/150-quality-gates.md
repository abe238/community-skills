# Quality Gates

Before finalizing, verify:

- Did I clearly separate consensus, debate, open questions, prerequisites, gaps, and my own inference?
- Did I use epistemic labels where they improve clarity: `[Consensus]`, `[Debate]`, `[Open]`, `[Prereq]`, `[Inference]`, `[Gap]`?
- Did I map the field rather than summarize chapters?
- Did I identify hidden assumptions?
- Did I avoid fake certainty when the corpus was weak?
- Did I generate genuinely diagnostic questions?
- Did I keep recommendations proportional to the evidence?

If any answer is no, fix it before returning.
