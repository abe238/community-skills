# Routing and Boundaries

## Use this skill when
- there are multiple substantive sources
- the user wants deep understanding, not a surface recap
- the user wants debate structure, mental models, or tutoring
- the task is exam prep, onboarding, or domain mapping

## Do not use this skill when
- a normal summary would do
- the source set is too thin for synthesis and the user only needs a quick answer
- the task is mostly creative rather than source-grounded

## Corpus sufficiency checks

Minimum viable field map:
- at least 3 substantive sources, or
- 1 source plus many rich notes/transcripts, or
- a coherent body of documentation with internal cross-reference

Minimum viable debate map:
- at least 2 differing perspectives or methods appear in the corpus

Minimum viable tutor loop:
- enough material exists to judge answers against something better than vibes

If sufficiency is weak:
- label the weak spots with `[Gap]`
- provide a best-effort conceptual map
- do not invent confidence you did not earn
