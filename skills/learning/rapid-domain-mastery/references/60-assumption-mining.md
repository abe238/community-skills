# Assumption Mining

This is where the hidden trapdoor lives.

Find what experts assume that beginners often do not even realize is being assumed.

## Look for
- unstated mathematical prerequisites
- unspoken methodological norms
- default causal assumptions
- background historical context
- notation that appears 'obvious' only after long exposure
- tacit standards for what counts as evidence
- moves treated as legitimate without explanation

## Output structure
For each assumption:
- what the assumption is
- why experts treat it as obvious
- why beginners miss it
- what confusion it causes
- how to repair the gap fast

## Rule
This section is never optional in deep-learning mode.
