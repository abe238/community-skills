# Corpus Audit

Audit before synthesis.

## Required outputs
- major topics/subfields represented
- strongest foundational sources
- strongest frontier/disputed sources
- likely blind spots
- overloaded or shifting terms
- assumed prerequisites
- low-leverage material to defer

## Audit questions
- Which sources are teaching-oriented versus debate-oriented?
- Which sources are primary versus secondary?
- Where do authors disagree in definitions?
- Which materials are likely outdated relative to the rest?
- What prior math, methods, notation, or theory do these sources silently assume?

## Anti-pattern
Do not pretend a narrow or biased corpus represents the whole field.
